About Us

Welcome to Numerical Analysis website, your go-to resource for learning and exploring numerical analysis. Our mission is to provide clear, structured, and practical lessons to help students, researchers, and professionals develop a strong foundation in numerical methods.

This platform is created and maintained by a third-year student from the Applied Mathematics and Statistics (AMS) Department of the Institute of Technology of Cambodia (ITC). All lessons, materials, and resources are carefully curated to ensure accuracy and relevance in computational mathematics.

What We Offer

  • Comprehensive Lessons – Covering key topics such as root-finding, interpolation, numerical integration, differential equations, and more.
  • Interactive Examples – Hands-on coding implementations and problem-solving techniques.
  • Real-World Applications– Bridging theory with practical use in engineering, science, and technology.

Whether you're a student looking to enhance your skills or a professional applying numerical methods in real-world problems,Numerical TP website is here to support your learning journey.

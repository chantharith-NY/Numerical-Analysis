Welcome to Lesson part!

The AMS Department at ITC offers essential lessons in Numerical Analysis, focusing on algorithms and computational methods to solve real-world problems. Students will learn key concepts such as root-finding, numerical integration, and error analysis. These lessons, designed for those pursuing careers in engineering, data science, and applied mathematics, are owned and curated by the AMS Department of ITC to meet high academic standards.
